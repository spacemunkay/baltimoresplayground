Playground Every 2nd Friday

A monthly meetup in the main square at Broadway & Lancaster on the 2nd Friday of every month from 9-10pm. After a fair share of four square, double dutch, freeze tag, or hula hooping, the fun moves on to one of the local bars nearby.

Other Events

Additional meetups will occur in other neighborhoods and at some of the many festivals in Baltimore. We've had painting sessions, field trips to the Pinball Museum & Skyzone Trampoline Center, and are frequent attendees of Brews and Boardgames. During the summer, we meet on Monday evenings for hula hooping in Patterson Park.
